📋 Know Your Rights

Minimum Pay: $21.44/hr before tips (April 2026: $22.13/hr)

Tips: 100% yours — apps cannot keep any portion or count tips as base pay

Payment: At least every 7 days, with itemized statements

No Fees: Apps cannot charge you to receive your pay

Distance: You can set maximum delivery distance limits

Bathrooms: Restaurants must let you use their bathroom during pickup

Bags: Free insulated bag after 6 deliveries

Complaint: nyc.gov/DeliveryApps or call 311 — no immigration info needed
